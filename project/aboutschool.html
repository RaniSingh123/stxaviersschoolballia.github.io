ST. XAVIER'S SCHOOL

St. Xavier's school, a pioneer of Education and harbinger of scientific vision owes its singular distinction to the St. Xavier's Educational Society, Salempur,Deoria(U.P.) under Mr.S. Bhushan, the chairman. It was established on 15th Feburary 1993 in the heartland of Hindi to richly and fabulously thrive English studies under CBSE Curriculum with a view of awakening the intellects of student and situated on the Ballia- Gorakhpur main road. The other branch of the school, popularly known as the city Branch is situated on the state highway at Jalalpur just two Kms from the city. it's another branch: St.Xavier's school, Salempur, Deoria has been also affiliated to the CBSE New Delhi.

The school provides study facilities for the classes Nurssery to Std. XII. the foundation stone of the school was laid down by His Excellency Prof. Vishnjukant Shastri, the then Governor of U.p. on 10th june 2001. Moreover the huge new building was inaugurated by His majesty Bhairon Singh Shekhawat, the then Vice-President of india on 23rd March 2003 in presence of Hon'ble Mr. Chandra Shekhar, the former Prime Minister of india with many other High dignitaries.

Discipline and justice being our aim, we provide most up-to-date knowledge through scientific methods- under congenial and hygienic environment. The school is affiliated to the CBSE, New Delhi and runs on the CBSE syllabus introducing modern and innovative techniques updated from time to time for excellent academic achievement of the students.
