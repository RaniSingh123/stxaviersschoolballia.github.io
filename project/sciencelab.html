Facilities Provided By Xaviers
Science laboratories

The School has large well equipped laboratories for Physics, Chemistry , General Science etc. with ultra modern facilities . Science classes is conducted in laboratories in scientific ambience.

Maths Laboratories

A state of art Maths lab has been developed to have the skills in mathematics and mke it more interactive , Mathematics comes alive and learning becomes child play.

Smart Classes

The school has introduced anew method of study through smart clsses in order to provide students a theoretical as well as practical understanding by interactive animations and step-by-step explanations through diagrams on the LCD projectors screen. There are LCD Projectors in several classes to make learning more interesting and interactive and to provide a maximum coverage of all the subjects in accordance with the C.B.S.E curriculum.

The Golden Third Eyes

A number of closed circuit cameras have been installed in various important places like classrooms, hostel rooms , offices, laboratories and libraries to monitor the daily situations of everybody present in the school premises. The school and its time to time are fuly checked out by these cameras so as to ensure safety and security to the students.
